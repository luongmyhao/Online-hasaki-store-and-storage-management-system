System Admin Guide

Use this guide to learn about your role, and the tools available to you as a System Admin.

Set up Workplace

Customize your Workplace

Add your company name, a logo, and terms of use to your Workplace community.

Bring in your IT team

Share the Workplace Technical Resources with your IT team. They’ll find information on IT configuration, authentication, account management, and more.

Integrate with your favorite tools

Check out the Integrations Directory to connect Workplace with the tools you use every day.

Manage Users, Groups & Content

Add or deactivate users

Add or deactivate users via the People section of the Admin Panel, or automate this process by connecting Workplace to your identity provider.

Manage your groups

Manage your organization’s groups from the Groups section of the Admin Panel. Assign admin roles to colleagues to help you moderate your community.

Manage content

Promote posts by executives so they appear at the top of everyone’s News Feed. Remove content that violates your Workplace policy from the Reported Content page.

Monitor Safety and Engagement

Keep Employees Safe

Use Safety Check on Workplace to check on the safety of your employees during a crisis. Assign Safety Operators to provide help during crises.

Measure Engagement

Monitor the Reporting section of the Admin Panel to analyze Workplace adoption and engagement. Use these insights to run contests and campaigns for claim rates, content production, and Workplace education.
